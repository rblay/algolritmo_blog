The attackers of the 2019 Brasileirão can be divided into 4 different finishing profiles using two pieces of information:

-   Number of shots per 90 minutes (Shots/90 min)
-   Quality of each shot (xG/Shot)

![](<images/atacantes-1-scaled.png>)

**1. Fewer shots of lower quality**

Many of these players are wingers, so they do not get as many high-quality scoring opportunities. In this group, attackers have fewer than 2 shots per game. Some examples are:

-   Rossi (VAS)
-   A. Victor (BAH)
-   Clayson (COR)

-   ![](<images/20_7io9xsM.jpg>)

-   ![](<images/artur-bahia.jpg>)


**2. More shots of lower quality**

Players in this group take many shots per game, but they do not always manage to finish in high-probability scoring situations. Some examples are:

-   Éverton (GRE)
-   Marinho (SAN)
-   Vitinho (FLA)

-   ![](<images/2019-08-05t235113z_188660446_rc1335892e10_rtrmadp_3_soccer-brazil-gre-chp.jpg>)

-   ![](<images/20190828141023154567o.jpg>)


**3. Fewer shots of higher quality**

Here we have the classic box strikers. They generally finish in very dangerous areas, but on the whole do not manage many shots throughout matches. Some examples are:

-   W. Paulista (FOR)
-   Boselli (COR)
-   M. Ruben (CAP)

-   ![](<images/whatsapp-image-2019-10-09-at-21.47.09.jpeg>)

-   ![](https___timaoweb-com-br.exactdn.com_wp-content_uploads_2019_11_Boselli-Corinthians-3-x-2-Fortaleza.jpg)


**4. More shots of higher quality**

This group contains the most impactful and dangerous players. They generate many shots with a high probability of resulting in goals. "These players will score today!" Some examples are:

-   Gabigol (FLA)
-   B. Henrique (FLA)
-   A Caike (BAH)
